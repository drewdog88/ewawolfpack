About the Eastlake Wolfpack Association

Supporting Eastlake High School's athletic programs and student success

Our Mission

The Eastlake Wolfpack Association (EWA) is a non-profit organization that supports the various teams and clubs at Eastlake High School in Sammamish, Washington. It acts as a fundraising and administrative umbrella for about 23 booster clubs.

What We Provide

Member Education
Insurance Coverage
Tax Assistance
Communication Forum

Our Impact

EWA plays a vital role in fundraising and directs retained funds to scholarships, ensuring that financial barriers don't prevent talented student-athletes from participating in Eastlake's athletic programs.

Non-Profit Status

The Eastlake Wolfpack Association is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.
