Our Mission

The Eastlake Wolfpack Association is dedicated to supporting and enhancing the athletic programs at Eastlake High School. Through fundraising, volunteerism, and community engagement, we provide resources that help our student-athletes excel both in competition and in life.

How We Support Our Athletes

Equipment & Facilities

Providing state-of-the-art equipment and facility improvements to give our teams a competitive edge.

Academic Support

Supporting academic excellence through scholarships and educational resources for student-athletes.

Community Building

Creating a strong, supportive community that celebrates athletic achievement and school spirit.
